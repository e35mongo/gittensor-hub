# Hub Score vs Gittensor emissions (TAO)

Do **not** confuse product reputation with subnet emissions.

| Concept | Who controls it | What it means |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Gittensor emissions (TAO/Alpha)** | SN74 validators + `master_repositories.json` | Rewards for scored, merged work on **listed** repos |
| **Hub Score** | Gittensor Hub product | Reputation / eligibility rail for hub features (leaderboards, later bounties) |

Rules of thumb:

- Hub Score **never equals** TAO.
- Being merged on Hub does **not** guarantee emissions unless the repo is listed and validators score the work.
- Hub may be **absent** from the live registry at times (e.g. after a weight cut). Presence proof (landing, changelog, Discord replies) is how Hub re-earns a listing — see roadmap / `/presence`.
